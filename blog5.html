Control Diabetes Naturally Drink

Admin 30 June 2024

Introduction


In this blog, we will discuss a natural drink recipe that can help control diabetes. This drink includes ingredients that are known to help regulate blood sugar levels.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight.
  2. In the morning, strain the water into a pan.
  3. Add cinnamon powder and turmeric powder to the pan.
  4. Boil the mixture for 5 minutes.
  5. Let it cool down for a few minutes.
  6. Add apple cider vinegar and honey.
  7. Stir well and drink on an empty stomach.

Benefits:

  • Fenugreek seeds: Helps lower blood sugar levels.
  • Cinnamon powder: Increases insulin sensitivity.
  • Turmeric powder: Has anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Apple cider vinegar: Improves insulin sensitivity.
  • Honey: Provides a natural sweetener.

This natural drink can be a part of a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle to help manage diabetes. Always consult with a healthcare provider before making any changes to your diet or health regimen.
